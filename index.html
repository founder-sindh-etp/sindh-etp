About Us

Welcome to Sindh ETP

We are a platform dedicated to helping students prepare for their university entry exams. Our goal is to provide high-quality resources and practice tests to ensure that you are well-prepared to succeed in your exams..

Our website covers a wide range of subjects, including General Knowledge, Every Day Science, English, Pakistan Studies, Islamic Studies and more. We believe in making learning accessible and effective for everyone, with detailed explanations and practice questions for each topic.

This platform was started on 26th August 2024, and we are currently on version 2.5, constantly improving to serve you better.
